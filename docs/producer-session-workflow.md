# Producer Session Workflow

The Producer Session layer captures Fred's live creative development process for Season Two scripts.

Primary source input:

`data/season-two-exemplars/PD-Script Generator Refinement Project June 7-13.docx`

This document preserves Fred's live producer dialogue with ChatGPT and the refined Season Two script development process. It is not a parsing target yet and should not modify the current generation engines until the process has been reviewed and translated into stable rules.

## Preferred Workflow

Sunday seven-day planning sessions are the preferred creative workflow.

Each Sunday session should help Fred prepare seven complete rough scripts for the coming week. These scripts are not final publication copy. They are strong working drafts for Fred to revise, sharpen, rename, reframe, or replace.

The process should feel like improvisational jazz, not mechanical automation. Fred brings the melody and direction. The AI riffs and drafts. Fred listens, redirects, edits, and calls the next move. The strongest scripts emerge from dialogue, not one-click generation.

## Sunday Seven-Day Planning

During a Sunday planning session, Fred reviews the upcoming Proverbs chapters, candidate verses, available seeds, and possible episode directions for the week ahead.

The session should produce a working seven-day map and rough script set:

- seven selected or likely verses
- seven working title directions
- seven rough episode scripts
- translation notes
- theological directions
- hook possibilities
- story seed opportunities
- practical challenge directions
- notes for Fred's later revision

## Dialogue-Based Development

Individual scripts are developed through conversation.

Fred gives:

- verse
- translation
- title instinct
- hook idea
- theological direction
- story seed
- practical challenge direction

The AI then riffs and drafts around those materials. The first draft is expected to be provisional. It gives Fred something to test, interrupt, sharpen, and reshape.

Fred may then:

- redirect
- edit
- rename
- reframe
- replace
- tighten
- expand
- change the hook
- change the theological emphasis
- change the practical challenge

The AI responds by revising, tightening, and re-drafting until the piece has a useful Season Two shape.

## Editorial Authority

Human editorial judgment remains authoritative.

The AI can propose language, structure, titles, hooks, and applications, but Fred's discernment governs:

- biblical fit
- theological precision
- title accuracy
- voice fidelity
- story appropriateness
- pacing
- usefulness for the listener
- final publication readiness

The goal is not to remove Fred from the process. The goal is to make the producer session faster, richer, and more creatively responsive.

## Exemplar Value

Final scripts from these producer sessions become exemplars for future generation.

They should help future systems learn:

- how Fred moves from instinct to structure
- how raw titles become devotional frames
- how a verse becomes a spoken episode
- how theological clarity is refined through revision
- how hooks are tested and strengthened
- how Fred's voice differs from a generic devotional voice
- how the AI should behave as a responsive creative partner
