# Title Engine Strategy

The Title Engine generates title suggestions for weekly planning rows.

## Modes

### 1. Seed Titles

Seed titles are existing titles already present in the Idea Reservoir. These are primary because they originate from Fred's creative process.

The Title Engine must always preserve the original seed title.

### 2. Generated Variations

Generated variations are optional alternatives inspired by the seed title, verse theme, and suggested direction.

They should support creative expansion, not replace the original seed title.

## Editorial Shape

Title suggestions should reflect Proverbs Daily Editorial DNA:

- memorable
- conversational
- culturally aware
- slightly playful when appropriate
- wisdom-oriented
- free of church cliches

The engine should favor:

- phrase flips
- questions
- paradoxes
- cultural observations
- everyday language

## Rules

- Seed titles are primary.
- Generated titles are optional.
- Human selection remains authoritative.
- Do not generate devotional content.
- Do not generate scripts.
- Do not generate subtitles yet.

The purpose is creative expansion, not automation.
