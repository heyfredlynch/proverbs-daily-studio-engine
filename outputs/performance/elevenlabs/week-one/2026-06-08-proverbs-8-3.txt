Proverbs 8:3

By the gates at the entrance to the town, on the road leading in, she cries aloud.

Today’s Episode: Location, Location, Location

If you’ve ever talked to someone in real estate, you’ve probably heard the phrase:

“Location, location, location.”

Why?

Because if you want people to find you, your business, your product, or your service, you’ve got to be in the right place.

Success often comes down to placement.

And when it comes to wisdom…

Talk about product placement!

Wisdom was with God when He created the world. She understands something about positioning. So if you’re looking for a bit of wisdom to help you out in your journey today, take a look at her résumé and she’ll tell you right where she’s at located:

Did you catch that? Wisdom says she’s everywhere!

At the gates.

At the entrance.

At the crossroads.

At the places where people are coming and going.

At the places where decisions are being made.

I like to think of it like this right when you are at the precipice of a decision… At the brink of execution of an idea… Look up because

She (wisdom) already there anticipating your arrival and willing to partner with you in your next move.

Waiting.

Speaking.

Calling.

In other words, wisdom understands location.

She positions herself wherever life is happening.

Where choices are being made.

Where futures are being shaped.

Where people need direction.

If you’ve ever raised children, you know this feeling.

You tell them something for years.

Years.

Then one day a coach, a teacher, a friend, or a complete stranger says the exact same thing.

Suddenly your child looks at them like they’re a genius.

And you’re standing there thinking:

“I’ve been saying that the whole time!”

Wisdom understands that feeling.

Humanity has ignored her for centuries.

Yet every morning she shows up again.

At the gate.

At the crossroads.

At the entrance.

Still speaking.

Still available.

Still present.

Maybe today you’re discouraged because you fell that people aren’t listening.

Maybe you’ve been faithful.

Showing up.

Serving.

Teaching.

Loving.

Repeating the same life-giving truth over and over.

Don’t quit your post.

Wisdom doesn’t.

She stays in place because she knows that eventually someone will hear.

And when they do, it can change everything.

Wisdom’s power isn’t just in what she says.
It’s in where she stays.

God, help me stay faithful where You’ve strategically placed me.

Identify one place God has positioned you to influence others.

A workplace.

A family.

A friendship.

A ministry.

Instead of wishing for another location, be fully present and embody the assignment of that location today.
