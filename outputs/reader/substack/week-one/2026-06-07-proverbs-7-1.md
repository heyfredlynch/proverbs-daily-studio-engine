Proverbs 7:1

“My son, keep my words and treasure up my commandments with you.”

Today’s Episode: How Treasures Are Measured

**Sunday, 2026-06-07 | ESV**

Some treasures are priceless.

Not because they’re expensive.

Because they’re personal.

A child may treasure a worn-out stuffed animal.

A grandmother may treasure a faded photograph.

A husband may treasure a wedding ring.

To everyone else, those things might seem ordinary.

But pricelessness isn’t determined by the market.

It’s determined by the meaning.

And meaning comes from relationship.

Notice the language.

God doesn’t just say:

“Remember my commandments.”

He says:

“Treasure them.”

Store them up.

Keep them close.

Protect them.

Value them.

Why?

Because treasures are never measured by what they cost.

They’re measured by what they’re worth to the one holding them.

Beauty is in the eye of the beholder.

And treasure is measured the same way.

The value isn’t merely in the object.

The value is in the relationship.

That’s why one person can read Scripture and see ink on paper.

While another person reads the same words and finds life.

One sees information.

The other sees treasure.

Think about your phone.

Most of the pictures on it wouldn’t mean much to a stranger.

But to you?

That’s your child. That’s your spouse. That’s your family. That’s a memory you’ll never get back.

The value isn’t in the pixels.

The value is in the relationship.

The same is true with God’s Word.

The more you walk with Him, the more valuable His words become.

Not because the words changed.

Because the relationship deepened.

Maybe that’s why some Scriptures have carried you for years.

A verse that once seemed ordinary became precious.

A promise you once overlooked became a lifeline.

Over time, God’s Word stopped being something you read.

It became something you treasured.

And that’s a beautiful sign of spiritual maturity.

Because mature faith doesn’t merely collect verses.

It cherishes them.

## 🔥 Remember
Treasures are measured by the relationship, not the price tag.

## 🙏🏽 Prayer
God, teach me to treasure Your words above all else.

## 🎯 Today's Challenge
Identify one Scripture that has become precious to you.

Write it down.

Carry it with you today.

And ask yourself:

“What makes this verse so valuable to me?”

The answer may reveal something beautiful about your relationship with God.
